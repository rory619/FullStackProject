Strategy:
Gives a Buy/Sell signal based on RSI
If stock price falls below 30% of the SMA100(The average over the last 100 days), it sesnd a buy signal.
Similarly, if it breaches 70% of the SMA100 it send a sell signal.

The reasoning behind this is if it falls below 30% it suggest a strong bearish momentum, possibly pessimistic
When it rises above 70% it suggest strong bullish momentum, possibly overheating, meaning it is being overbought and thus is easy to sell for a good price.
